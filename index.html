What We Do

Comprehensive Manufacturing Solutions

Custom Injection Molding

How It Works

The Injection Molding Process

Watch how raw plastic pellets are transformed into precision-engineered components through our advanced injection molding process.

1
2
3
4
01

Clamping

The two halves of the mold are securely clamped together under high pressure by the hydraulic clamping unit, creating a sealed cavity.

02

Injection

Molten plastic is injected at high pressure through the nozzle into the mold cavity. The screw mechanism ensures consistent material flow.

03

Cooling

The molten plastic cools and solidifies within the mold, taking on the precise shape of the cavity with uniform temperature distribution.

04

Ejection

Once solidified, the mold opens and ejector pins push the finished part out. The cycle is complete and ready to repeat with precision.
