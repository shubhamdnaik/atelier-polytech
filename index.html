Who We Are

Crafting Precision, Delivering Excellence

At Atelier Polytech, we combine decades of engineering expertise with cutting-edge technology to transform raw plastic into precision-engineered components.

Our Story

Founded with a vision to bring agility and modern technology to plastic manufacturing, Atelier Polytech is an ambitious new player in the industry. Our state-of-the-art facility in Bangalore houses the latest injection molding machines capable of producing everything from micro-precision medical components to durable automotive parts.

As a dynamic startup, we break away from the rigid processes of legacy manufacturers. We offer personalized engineering support, rapid prototyping, and a commitment to quality that ensures your ideas are transformed into reality with unmatched precision.

What We Do

Comprehensive Manufacturing Solutions

End-to-end injection molding services tailored to your unique requirements, from initial concept through full-scale production.

Custom Injection Molding

High-volume and low-volume production runs using a wide range of thermoplastic and thermosetting materials, tailored to your exact specifications.

Mold Design & Engineering

Expert CAD/CAM mold design with DFM analysis, flow simulation, and precision tooling to ensure optimal part quality and cycle efficiency.

Prototyping & Testing

Rapid prototyping with soft tooling for design validation, functional testing, and market evaluation before committing to production tooling.

Overmolding & Insert Molding

Multi-material and insert molding capabilities for complex assemblies combining metal, plastic, and rubber in a single component.

Assembly & Post-Processing

Complete finishing services including pad printing, hot stamping, ultrasonic welding, painting, and mechanical assembly for turnkey solutions.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality control with CMM inspection, dimensional analysis, and material testing ensuring every part meets your exact specifications.
