Our Manufacturing Services

Complete Injection Molding Solutions

End-to-end plastic injection molding services in Bangalore, India. From custom mold design and rapid prototyping to high-volume production and quality assurance - we handle all your plastic manufacturing needs.

Custom Injection Molding

High-volume and low-volume plastic injection molding production runs using thermoplastic and thermosetting materials. Custom plastic parts manufactured to your exact specifications with tight tolerances and superior quality control.

Mold Design & Engineering

Expert CAD/CAM mold design services with DFM analysis, flow simulation, and precision tooling. Our experienced mold designers ensure optimal part quality, reduced cycle time, and cost-effective injection molding production.

Rapid Prototyping Services

Fast prototyping with soft tooling for design validation, functional testing, and market evaluation. Get physical prototypes in days before committing to expensive production tooling and full-scale manufacturing.

Overmolding & Insert Molding

Multi-material and insert molding capabilities for complex assemblies combining metal, plastic, and rubber in a single component.

Assembly & Post-Processing

Complete finishing services including pad printing, hot stamping, ultrasonic welding, painting, and mechanical assembly for turnkey solutions.

Quality Assurance

Rigorous quality control with CMM inspection, dimensional analysis, and material testing ensuring every part meets your exact specifications.
